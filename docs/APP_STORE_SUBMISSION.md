## Submission Steps

1. **Archive Your App** in Xcode
   - Product ‚Üí Archive
   - Wait for build to complete

2. **Distribute to App Store Connect**
   - Window ‚Üí Organizer ‚Üí Archives
   - Select your archive ‚Üí Distribute App
   - Choose "App Store Connect"
   - Upload

3. **Complete App Store Connect**
   - Log into appstoreconnect.apple.com
   - Select your app
   - Fill in all information from this guide
   - Upload screenshots
   - Add app privacy details
   - Save

4. **Submit for Review**
   - Click "Submit for Review"
   - Answer export compliance questions
   - Submit

5. **Wait for Review**
   - Typical review time: 24-48 hours
   - Check email for updates
   - Respond to any questions from Apple
