# Privacy Policy for NFL Outcome Predictor

**Last Updated: December 1, 2024**

## Overview

NFL Outcome Predictor ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the iOS application.

## Information Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

The App operates entirely using publicly available data sources and does not require user accounts, registration, or any personal information.

## Data Sources

The App retrieves data from these public sources:

### ESPN Public API
- Team schedules and rosters
- Live game scores
- Historical game results
- Team statistics

**Purpose**: Display current and historical NFL game information.

### The Odds API
- Betting lines and odds
- Point spreads
- Over/under totals

**Purpose**: Display betting odds for informational and entertainment purposes. The App does not facilitate gambling or betting.

## Data Usage

All data fetched from public APIs is:
- Used only for display within the App
- Not stored permanently on your device
- Not shared with third parties
- Not used for analytics or tracking

## Device Permissions

The App requires:
- **Internet Connection**: Required to fetch live game data and predictions from our servers.

The App does NOT request:
- Location services
- Camera or photo access
- Contacts or calendar access
- Push notifications (optional future feature)

## Third-Party Services

### Our Prediction Server
The App connects to our server (hosted on Azure) to generate AI-powered predictions. This server:
- Does not log or store user data
- Does not track user behavior
- Only processes prediction requests in real-time
- Does not require authentication

### No Analytics
The App does not use analytics services (no Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, etc.).

### No Advertising
The App does not display advertisements or use ad networks.

## Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 or any users.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to secure. All communications with our prediction server use standard HTTPS encryption.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data:
- There is no data to request, modify, or delete
- You are not tracked across websites or apps
- Your usage of the App is completely anonymous
