# StatShark Branding Guide

## Brand Identity

**Name:** StatShark
**Tagline:** AI-Powered Sports Predictions
**Mission:** Empower fantasy sports players and sports enthusiasts with data-driven predictions

## Logo & Icon

**Primary Icon:** Football with shark theme
- Blue gradient background (#1a237e to #0d47a1)
- Brown football with white laces
- Green chart bars (prediction indicator)
- Subtle basketball/baseball elements for multi-sport

**Shark Mascot:** "Bull Shark" (error handling character)
- Yellow nose ring
- Friendly but sharp
- Represents data precision

## Typography

**Primary Font:** SF Pro (iOS system font)
- Display/Headlines: SF Pro Display Bold
- Body Text: SF Pro Text Regular
- Data/Stats: SF Pro Text Medium

**Numerical Data:** Tabular numbers for alignment

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Personality
- **Smart but approachable** - Data-driven without being intimidating
- **Confident but honest** - Clear about predictions, transparent about uncertainty
- **Fun but professional** - Fantasy focus with serious analytics

### Writing Style
- Use "we" and "you" (conversational)
- Lead with benefits, support with data
- Short sentences, active voice
- Sports analogies welcome
- Shark puns: sparingly, tastefully

### Examples

‚úÖ **Good:**
- "Dominate your fantasy league with StatShark"
- "AI-powered predictions you can trust"
- "See the game before it's played"

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- "We're the best prediction app ever!" (too boastful)
- "Guaranteed wins!" (unrealistic promises)
- "Sharktastic predictions!" (too punny)

## Competitor Positioning

### vs FiveThirtyEight
- **Their strength:** Political predictions, broad coverage
- **Our advantage:** Mobile-first, fantasy-focused, real-time

### vs ESPN Fantasy
- **Their strength:** Official NFL partner, huge user base
- **Our advantage:** AI predictions, better UX, cross-sport

### vs Betting Apps
- **Their strength:** Real-time odds, large marketing budgets
- **Our difference:** Fantasy focus, no gambling, educational
