StatShark Support

Welcome to StatShark support. Find answers to common questions and learn how to get the most out of the app.

Getting Started

Q: Do I need to create an account?
No! StatShark works immediately after download. No registration, no login, no personal information required.

Q: How do I view team information?
Open the app and tap the "Teams" tab. Select any of the 32 NFL teams to view their roster, schedule, and latest news.

Q: How do predictions work?
Tap the "Predict" tab to see AI-powered predictions for upcoming games. Each prediction includes a confidence score and reasoning. You can also create custom predictions by tapping "Predict a Game" and selecting any two teams.

Fantasy Football

Q: How do I build a fantasy team?
Tap the "Fantasy" tab, then "Team Search". Select a team to browse players, or select a position filter (QB, RB, WR, TE, K, DEF) to see top players across all teams. Tap any player to add them to your roster.

Q: What are the roster limits?
- Quarterbacks (QB): 2 max
- Running Backs (RB): 3 max
- Wide Receivers (WR): 3 max
- Tight Ends (TE): 2 max
- Kickers (K): 1 max
- Defense (DEF): 1 max

Q: How do I change my team name?
In the Fantasy tab, tap the gear icon (⚙️) in the top left to access settings. From there you can edit your team name and league names.

Q: How do fantasy leagues work?
Create a league, share the invite code with friends, and they can join using that code. All league members can view each other's rosters and compete for the best fantasy team.

Game Information

Q: How do I see live scores?
Live games appear in the "Predict" tab and on team detail pages. Games in progress show a red "LIVE" indicator with current score, quarter, and time remaining.

Q: Can I view past games?
Yes! On any team's detail page, use the season selector to view games from 2020-2025. Historical matchup data is also shown on game detail pages.

Q: Where do the player stats come from?
Player statistics come from API-Sports and are real season data (not sample data). Stats are available from the 2022 season onwards.

Troubleshooting

Q: The app shows "Error Loading Data"
Make sure you have an internet connection. StatShark requires network access to fetch live NFL data. Try tapping the refresh button or pulling down to refresh the content.

Q: Player photos are not loading
Player headshots are loaded from external sources and may take a moment to appear. If photos don't load, check your internet connection.

Q: Predictions are not available
Predictions require an active internet connection to our API server. If predictions are unavailable, the server may be updating or experiencing high traffic. Try again in a few minutes.

Q: News articles won't open
News articles open in Safari. Make sure Safari is not restricted in your device settings (Settings → Screen Time → Content & Privacy Restrictions).

Data & Privacy

Q: What data does StatShark collect?
None! StatShark does not collect any personal data. Your fantasy team and preferences are stored locally on your device only. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: Can I use the app offline?
Previously viewed content may be available offline, but StatShark requires an internet connection to fetch live scores, predictions, and updated data.
